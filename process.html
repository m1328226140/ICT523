This is the investigative process

The process is as follows：    
First, we need to design the questionnaire;    
Then send a link to the questionnaire to the customer;    
Third, invite customers to fill in the information;    
Fourth, collect all the questionnaires distributed;    
Then analyze according to the content filled in;    
Finally, the questionnaire is summarized and reported.    
The above are the steps and process of the investigation.
